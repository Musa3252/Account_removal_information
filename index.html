Request permanent deletion of your axcebded account and personal data. This page is public and does not require sign-in.

Overview

When you request deletion, we disable your account and begin removing your personal data after a short grace period. Some limited records may be retained where required by law (for example, billing or security logs).

You can also request deletion in-app: Profile → Settings → Account → Delete account.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, login, and personal settings.
  • Personal content stored only in your account space (e.g., drafts, private notes).
  • Connected devices and active sessions are revoked.

What may remain

  • Content others still rely on (e.g., messages in shared threads) to preserve conversation history.
  • Minimal records required for legal, fraud-prevention, or billing purposes.
  • Encrypted backups purge on their normal rotation schedule.

Timeline

  • Request: Submit the form below. We may ask you to verify via email.
  • Grace period: Account is disabled for a short period (typically 7–30 days). Signing in during this time may cancel deletion.
  • Permanent removal: After the grace period, your account and eligible data are permanently deleted. Backups purge on their cycle.

Before you delete

  • Export any data you want to keep.
  • Cancel active subscriptions to avoid future charges.
  • Make sure you’re requesting deletion for the correct account/email.
