Account Deletion Information

This page explains how deleting your account works. You cannot delete your account here — please use the mobile app to complete deletion.

Overview

Deleting your account permanently removes your profile and most personal data after a short grace period. Some limited records may be kept where required by law. You can find the Delete account option inside the app: Profile → Settings → Account → Delete account.

axcebded

The information on this page applies to the axcebded app experience. If you have multiple accounts, you must request deletion for each account separately inside the app.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, login credentials, and personal settings.
  • Personal content stored only in your account space (e.g., drafts, private notes).
  • Connected devices and active sessions are revoked.

What may remain

  • Content shown to others in shared contexts (e.g., messages in group threads) may remain to preserve conversation history.
  • Minimal records required for legal, fraud‑prevention, or billing purposes (e.g., invoices, security logs) may be retained for a limited time.
  • Data in encrypted backups is removed on their regular rotation schedule.

Timeline

  • Request: Start deletion inside the app. We may ask you to confirm via email or in‑app.
  • Grace period: Your account is disabled for a short period (typically 7–30 days). Signing in during this time can cancel deletion.
  • Permanent removal: After the grace period, your account and eligible data are permanently deleted. Backups purge on their cycle.

Before you delete

  • Export any data you want to keep.
  • Cancel subscriptions to avoid future charges.
  • Ensure you are signed in to the correct account on your device.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the axcebded app and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings → Account.
  3. Choose Delete account and follow the prompts.

If you don’t see the option, update the app to the latest version.

FAQs

Can I reverse deletion?

If a grace period is available, sign in again before it ends to cancel deletion. After permanent removal, it cannot be undone.

How long will it take?

Most deletions complete within 7–30 days after confirmation. Backup removal may take longer based on rotation schedules.

Need help?

Contact support from inside the app (Profile → Help & support) or email support@example.com.
